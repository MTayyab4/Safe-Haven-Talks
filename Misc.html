Maybe you're worried about being judged or labeled. Maybe you think you should be able to handle everything on your own. But here's the thing: you're not alone. Millions of people around the world deal with mental health challenges. It doesn't make you weak, it makes you human. Alright, so let's talk about some specific things you might be experiencing. Maybe you're feeling overwhelmed with anxiety, like your mind is constantly racing and your body feels tense.
